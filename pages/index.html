How to Play

Fifteen Plus is a spin-off of the 15-puzzle. The objective of this game to solve a 4x4, 5x5, 6x6, and 7x7 board in the least amount of time.

You may choose which board to begin with, and three boards that come after will be ordered from smallest to largest. To solve a board, you must arrange the numbers in ascending order like so:

You can move tiles into the adjacent empty square by using WASD and your arrow keys.

It's surprisingly difficult at first to even solve the 4x4, but don't let that discourage you! The pattern to solve a board becomes clear with a bit of practice.

Hint: Try arranging 1-2-3-4-5-9-13 first!
