How to Play

16253649! is a spin-off of 15-puzzle. In this game, the objective is to solve a 4x4, 5x5, 6x6, and 7x7 board as fast as possible.

You may choose what board to begin with, and three boards that come after will be random. To solve a board, you must arrange the numbers in ascending order starting from 1 to the last number like so:

You can move tiles into the adjacent empty square by clicking on it or using WASD and your arrow keys.

It's surprisingly difficult at first to even solve the 4x4, but don't let that discourage you! The move input pattern becomes very clear with a bit of practice.
